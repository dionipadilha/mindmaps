
TRIGONOMETRIC  FUNCTIONS

| Function | Definitions     | Symmetry | T  | Domain                                | Range   |
|----------|-----------------|----------|----|---------------------------------------|---------|
| sin(x)   | co / hyp        | Odd      | 2π | Reals                                 | [-1, 1] |
| cos(x)   | ca / hyp        | Even     | 2π | Reals                                 | [-1, 1] |
| tan(x)   | sin(x) / cos(x) | Neither  | π  | Reals, excluding odd multiples of π/2 | Reals   |
